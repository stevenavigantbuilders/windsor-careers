Windsor Estates is the flagship brand of the Windsor Group of Companies, founded by Danny Sawh — a Guyanese entrepreneur whose Stonewall Contracting Corp. delivered over US$200 million in public works contracts across New York City.

In 2013, that expertise came home. Windsor Estates at Peter's Hall became Guyana's first Florida-style gated community — 40 acres of single-family homes, townhomes, and condominiums, protected by 24/7 armed security and governed by standards that protect every owner's investment.

Our homeowners have seen property values appreciate by as much as 300%. We are the preferred address for oil & gas expatriates, diaspora investors, and discerning Guyanese families.
